Rules

  • There are only 3 tigers, 15 goats, and 23 positions

  • 3 Tigers initially placed into hrn, c2 and c3 positions

  • 15 goats enter one by one and then they can also move

  • Goats and Tigers can move to next right, left, top, down positions but not across

  • Tigers can kill adjacent Goats by jumping next to it, so this "jumping to" location should be empty

  • Alternate moves for characters

  • Goats should try to block all tigers

  • Tigers should try to kill Goats, and be on the move

  • If 5 goats are killed then it is considered as a win for tigers, because 10 goats are too few to block all tigers

  • More goats on the field, more chances are there for goats to win

  • UNDO is not possible
